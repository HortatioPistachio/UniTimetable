Welcome to the UofA Timetable converter

This web app takes your Uni timetable and puts it into your google calendar. During the process, the only data collected is the timetable that gets shared with you. All passwords are entered into the Universities system. You can check this by looking at the URL, if it ends with "adelaide.edu.au" the university owns it. The code is open source and more can be found in the about tab
