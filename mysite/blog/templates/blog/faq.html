FAQ

When I click the link for step 2, the page is empty?

This likely means that you haven't enrolled in any classes for the current year

I'm not receiving an email?

Make sure that the email address you are providing is a google account. If you can log into gmail with it, it's a google account. Also ensure that you have typed in your email correctly.

My timetable has changed since I last used this service?

Unfortunately, this service will not update your timetable. To get your most up to date timetable you must delete your old calendar and then run the service again. Instructions on how to delete a calendar in your google account can be found here.

I can't find my problem here?

Send us an email describing what's happening at conor.barry@student.adelaide.edu.au , I will do my best to help
